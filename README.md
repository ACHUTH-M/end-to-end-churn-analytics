## Objective
Identify drivers of churn and provide actionable business insights.

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month customers churn at 42.7%
- Fiber optic customers show high churn (41.9%)
- Low-tenure customers are high risk
- Higher monthly charges increase churn probability

## Model Performance
- Accuracy: 80%
- AUC: 0.836
- Recall improved from 57% to 76% using threshold tuning

## Business Impact
Model can be used to proactively target high-risk customers for retention campaigns.
